SMART RETAIL ANALYTICS ENGINE — RETAIL POLICY DOCUMENT
Version 1.0 | Effective: January 2024

=============================================================
SECTION 1: RETURN AND REFUND POLICY
=============================================================

Customers may return any product within 30 days of purchase with a valid receipt.
Items must be in original, unused condition with all original packaging intact.
Electronics may be returned within 15 days of purchase provided they are unopened.
Clothing items may be exchanged or returned within 30 days if tags are still attached.
Groceries and perishable items are non-returnable once purchased.
Furniture returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery for manufacturing defects only.
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.
Store credit is issued immediately for returns without a receipt.
Automotive parts may only be returned if unused and in original packaging within 14 days.
Health and Beauty products are non-returnable once opened for hygiene reasons.

=============================================================
SECTION 2: PROMOTIONAL AND DISCOUNT POLICY
=============================================================

Promotional discounts may not be combined with other offers unless explicitly stated.
Seasonal promotions are valid only during the advertised promotional period.
Employee discounts are capped at 20% and apply to full-price items only.
Clearance items are sold as-is and are not eligible for further discounts.
Loyalty program members earn 1 point per dollar spent; 100 points equals $1 store credit.
Flash sale prices are valid for 24 hours only and cannot be price-matched after expiry.
Discount codes must be applied at checkout and cannot be applied retroactively.
Bundle discounts apply only when all items in the bundle are purchased together.
Promotional pricing does not apply to gift cards, warranties, or service fees.

=============================================================
SECTION 3: INVENTORY AND STOCK MANAGEMENT POLICY
=============================================================

Minimum stock levels must be maintained for all high-velocity SKUs at all times.
Reorder points are calculated based on 14-day rolling average sales velocity.
Stockouts must be reported to the inventory manager within 24 hours of detection.
Overstock situations exceeding 90 days of supply trigger an automatic markdown review.
Dead stock (no sales in 180 days) is flagged for liquidation or donation.
Seasonal inventory must be ordered at least 60 days before the season start date.
Cross-store transfers are permitted to balance stock levels across regions.
All inventory adjustments must be logged with a reason code in the system.
Shrinkage above 2% of total inventory value triggers a mandatory audit.

=============================================================
SECTION 4: PRICING POLICY
=============================================================

All prices are set by the central pricing team and must not be altered at store level.
Price matching is offered for identical products sold by authorized competitors.
Price changes take effect at the start of the next business day after approval.
Markdown schedules for seasonal items follow a 10%/20%/30% tiered reduction plan.
Prices must always reflect applicable taxes and fees in the displayed amount.
Dynamic pricing adjustments are permitted for perishables within a 15% variance band.
All pricing data is audited monthly for compliance with regional regulations.

=============================================================
SECTION 5: STORE OPERATIONS POLICY
=============================================================

Store opening hours are 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM Monday through Saturday.
Sunday hours are 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM.
All cash registers must be reconciled at the end of each shift.
Customer complaints must be escalated to a supervisor within 10 minutes.
Loss prevention protocols require bag checks at all store exits.
Staff must complete mandatory compliance training annually.
CCTV footage is retained for a minimum of 30 days.
Emergency evacuation drills are conducted quarterly.

=============================================================
SECTION 6: REGIONAL SALES GUIDELINES
=============================================================

North Region: Focus on Electronics and Automotive categories; winter seasonal promotions run October through February.
South Region: Groceries and Health categories are highest velocity; summer promotions run May through August.
East Region: Clothing and Beauty are top performers; back-to-school promotions run July through September.
West Region: Sports and Outdoor categories lead; promotions align with outdoor activity seasons.
Central Region: Furniture and Books are primary categories; promotions tied to home improvement seasons.
Northeast Region: Electronics and Clothing dominate; holiday promotions are the highest revenue period.
Northwest Region: Sports and Groceries are primary; eco-friendly product lines are prioritized.
Southeast Region: Beauty and Health lead; warm-weather promotions run year-round.
Southwest Region: Automotive and Sports are top categories; desert climate drives seasonal demand patterns.

=============================================================
SECTION 7: DATA AND ANALYTICS POLICY
=============================================================

All sales data must be uploaded to the analytics platform within 24 hours of close of business.
Customer data is anonymized before any analytics processing.
Demand forecasts are generated weekly using the RandomForest model.
Anomaly detection runs daily on the previous day's sales data.
Model retraining is triggered when R2 score drops below 0.70 on the validation set.
All model artifacts are versioned and stored in the model vault.
Access to raw sales data is restricted to authorized analytics personnel.
Reports generated from the platform may be shared internally but not externally without approval.
